My Journey

My introduction to web development

It all started with a conversation with Nuno, my new neighbor in back in the summer of 2020. A developer for over 25 years, he described his job to me with passion and enthusiasm. This left an impression on me, and I was curious to learn more.

My curiousity has led me to prepare for and pass 5 Brainbench certifications, read several books, watch a ton of youtube videos, and plan and code this website. This has been my learning tool and personal coding playground. I am constantly tweaking things as I continue to improve.

I am currently taking the online C# Mastercourse by Tim Corey. His teaching style is practical, and he makes you put in the practice. He is an excellent teacher because he actually makes me love programming which is something I never thought I would admit.

The creative side in me has a very strong interest in seriously learning CSS3. I can envision myself becoming an expert at it because I'm a bit obsessive when it comes to creating the perfect layout.

I plan to start a new career for a great company who understands that my passion to become an excellent developer exceeds my current lack of qualifications. If you want someone who is teachable, consistent, highly driven, and always looking to improve, I'm your guy.
